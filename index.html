About
I am a final year undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering at IIT Mandi. My research interests largely focus on optimal control theory and would like to explore theoretical reinforcement learning in future. I am fortunate to be advised by Sriram Sankararaman (UCLA), Serghei Mangul (USC) and Dileep A.D. (IIT Mandi). I also closely collaborate with Rayan Chikhi (Institut Pasteur) and Eran Halperin (UCLA). I am supported by National Talent Search Examination Fellowship which I was awarded in 2017.

In my undergraduate, I have worked on multiple projects which spans accross statistical learning, computational geometry, data science, computer vision and natural language processing.
